# Workflows

## Seller Intake

1. Receive website form.
2. Validate and normalize contact/property fields.
3. Capture consent and attribution.
4. Detect duplicate contact/property/lead candidates.
5. Create or update contact, property, and lead records.
6. Check suppression before communication.
7. Create speed-to-lead task.
8. Queue AI extraction or summary after records are stored.

## Underwriting

1. Create underwriting version.
2. Retrieve provider data through adapter.
3. Store raw and normalized facts.
4. Search and score comps.
5. Human reviews comp set.
6. Calculate ARV range, repair estimate, and offer scenarios.
7. Human approves ARV, repairs, and offer ceiling.

## Compensation

1. Record collected revenue.
2. Apply direct deal deductions.
3. Calculate net commissionable revenue.
4. Apply effective-dated rules.
5. Calculate acquisition, disposition, founder override, and company share.
6. Report monthly advertising separately.
